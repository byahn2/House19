The Glass City

Along the coast of Downstairs is a narrow country called The Cabinet of Liquor. The capital of this city is Wine Cellar, but people mainly visit to see the Glass City. On weekends, the city floods with tourists who come for wild nights in the city's many clubs and casinos. Sometimes they leave with fantastic memories and good times.
