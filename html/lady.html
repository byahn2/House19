Lady Luminance

At the tip of the United Kingdoms of Bedroom is the statue known as Lady Lumiance. Bedroom is known as a land of knowledge, exploration, and opportunity. Itâ€™s divided into five kingdoms: Dresser, Desk , Bed , Shelf , and Muse , each of which is dedicated to a particular study or art. People from all over the world travel to Bedroom in search of an opportunity to create a new life for themselves, and the first thing they see as they enter at the bay of Shelf is Lady Luminance standing over them, holding her light like a beacon of hope.

Unlike most people, I took this photograph as I was leaving Bedroom to explore and photograph Downstairs and Basement. I felt that she was waving me goodbye and wishing me luck on my travels, reminding me that I would be welcome whenever I chose to return.
